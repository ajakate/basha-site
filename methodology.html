All About Spaced

Repetition Learning (SRL)

The Most Effective Memorization Technique

Since it's inception in 1939, cognitive psychologists have consistently proven the theory's effectiveness. One of the most recent studies show that SRL can increase knowledge retention by 200%, with all other factors controlled.

The Science

Our integrations use a spaced repetition algorithm that increases time intervals between phrases. The algorithm outsmarts the 'forgetting curve' to strategically reinforce neural connenctions. Anki goes beyond flashcards by taking the brainwork out of informational spacing.
