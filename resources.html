Sentence Lists

With Basha, you can open a text editor and create any list of phrases you want translated! While that flexibility is amazing, it can also be quite daunting building out lists on your own.

With that in mind, we've assembled a collection of sentence lists that you can upload directly to Basha. These should work with any language.

These sentences comprise the curriculum used by Speechling (a fantastic free SRS product), and contain enough varied sentences for you to become fluent.

Community Decks

Basha is entirely community-driven; that's why we describe ourselves as P2P! Learning a new language is no fun if you don't have anyone to speak it with. That is why we encourage users to contribute their decks with us so they can give back to the community and earn some much-deserved recognition here.
Be sure to add the tag "basha" when you share so others can find your decks.
